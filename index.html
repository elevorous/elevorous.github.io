Prototype: VariegataProto.

Controls

  • Left and Right arrow keys to move
  • Space to jump. Holding space longer results in a higher jump.
  • To toggle the Profiler Shift+P. While the profiler is open:
    • F5 toggles VSync (you will not see a difference in FPS for this browser deployment, but the static memory should jump up)
    • F6 toggles Low CPU mode

Proto Gameplay

  • Health and Lives are shown in the upper left corner. If you health reaches 0, you lose a life. If you lose all lives, it's Game Over
  • Complete the level by reachin the endpoint with a LevelKey.
  • Collecting seeds alters your defense against certain surfaces, cutting damage taken by up to 50%.
  • Certain surfaces cause instant death depending on your defense weighting.
